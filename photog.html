What story does your photo tell?

"This picture shows how dedicated every student who attends SMMC is. In this image, we see that no matter how hot it gets, they make the effort to ride their bicycles just to get to school and to return home safely to their families. These are the kinds of students who should be emulated, as they work hard in their daily lives, regardless of the difficulties and hardships they face each day." It reflects the familiar rhythm of daily travel and the humble reality.
